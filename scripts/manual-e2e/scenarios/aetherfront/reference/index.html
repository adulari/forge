Field Manual

Command

LMB select or drag a box. Hold Shift to add. RMB moves or attacks. A then click for attack-move. H holds.

Economy

Harvesters automatically mine cyan crystal and return it to your Bastion or Refinery. Build power plants to avoid 50% slower production.

Construction

Press B or use the build menu. Preview snaps to the grid. R rotates; right-click or Esc cancels.

Victory

Capture relays by standing nearby for income and vision. Destroy the red Citadel. Protect your own—its loss is final.
